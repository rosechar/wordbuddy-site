Word Buddy — Support

We're happy to help.

Need a hand? Email crose2016@gmail.com and we'll get back to you. Word Buddy is a word-guessing game made for young readers — here are answers to the most common questions.

How do you play?

Guess the hidden word in six tries. Type a word and press ENTER, and the tiles change colour to give you clues:

  • Green — right letter, right spot.
  • Yellow — right letter, wrong spot.
  • Gray — that letter isn't in the word.

There's a "How to Play" guide inside the app too, in the ••• menu.

How do hints work?

After a couple of guesses, a Hint button appears. Tap it for a kid-friendly clue — one per turn. If your child is really stuck after several guesses, a "Starts with?" helper can reveal the first letter. You can also tap the speaker icons to hear words and clues read aloud.

What's the difference between Today and Practice?

Today is one shared daily puzzle — solve it to build a "days in a row" streak, with a calendar to look back on. Practice is endless free play; pick Easy (4 letters), Medium (5), or Hard (6) and get a new word whenever you like.

Can I make my own puzzle?

Yes! In the ••• menu choose "Make a puzzle for a friend", pick a secret word and add your own clues, then pass the device for someone to solve.

How do I turn the daily reminder on or off?

Open the ••• menu and toggle Daily reminder. It's off until you turn it on, and turning it off cancels the reminder. You can also manage notifications in iOS Settings.

Accessibility options

In the ••• menu you'll find Colour-friendly tiles (adds a shape to each colour for colour-blind players) and Sound & vibration. Word Buddy also supports VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, and Reduce Motion.

Is my child's data collected?

No. Word Buddy collects no personal information, has no ads or analytics, and doesn't connect to the internet — everything stays on the device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

My stats or streak disappeared

All progress is stored only on your device, so deleting and reinstalling the app (or moving to a new device) will reset stats, streaks, and history. There are no accounts or cloud backups.
